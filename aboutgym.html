About Fit And Fine

Fitness And Fine Gym is a unisex fitness club established on 1st Aug 2014.We offer different packages to help you to achieve your fitness objective and also to add spice in your workout by combining fitness with fun. At Fitness And Fine Gym you will find excellent ambiance along with a team of certified trainers who are always ready to help and scientifically designed equipment and workout schedules designed to help you to achieve your fitness objective by minimizing the risk of injuries.Since during the peak hours,we have five trainers no the floor (including both ladies and gents). We try and maintain a ratio of one trainer is to four – five members so that everyone gets proper attention. To add up spice in your cardio workout, we have introduced a wide range of group activities including Yoga, Power Yoga, Aerobics, Fusion, Bollywood, Zumba, Bhangda, Cardio Kickboxing etc. For each of these batches, there would be an expert on that particulate fiend to conduct the batch.
